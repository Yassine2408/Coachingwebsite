Frequently Asked Questions

What is coaching?

Coaching is a collaborative process that helps individuals or teams identify and achieve their personal or professional goals.

How long does a typical coaching engagement last?

The duration varies based on individual needs, but typically ranges from 3 to 6 months with sessions every 2-4 weeks.

Is coaching confidential?

Yes, all coaching sessions and communications are kept strictly confidential.
